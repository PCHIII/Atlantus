The Problem

Athletic programs and NIL collectives face intense pressure to fund scholarships, equipment, travel, and athlete support services - all while navigating donor fatigue and competition for attention.

The proposed NIL settlement will require athletic departments to share a significant portion of revenue with athletes - up to 22% in some cases. This shift, combined with donor fatigue and rising compliance costs, puts pressure on budgets, especially for non-revenue sports. To sustain broad athletic offerings and competitive performance, departments must identify new, scalable revenue streams beyond traditional sources.

Traditional fundraising methods often fall short: limited reach, seasonal appeal, and administrative burden leave opportunities on the table.

A new approach is needed, one that connects with supporters where they already are: online, shopping, and eager to support their favorite teams and athletes.

The Solution

eComNowTeams enables Athletic Departments and NIL Collectives to launch branded online stores that drive revenue 24/7 - without asking donors for more money.

Supporters shop exclusive deals and branded merchandise, and a portion of each purchase goes back to the program or collective - it's simple, sustainable, and automated.

From gift cards to co-branded merchandise, this model creates a new fundraising engine built for the modern fan and donor.
